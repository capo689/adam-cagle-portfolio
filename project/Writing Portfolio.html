Sunset Marquis
ManifestoRelaunchWebPrint

West Hollywood rock-and-roll hotel. Relaunched with new Villas, Spa, and restaurant — without losing an ounce of the mythology. Netty Award for the site.

Manifesto · Opening spread

Every rock star of the last forty years slept here. The question is never whether it's legendary. Only what you'll do with the night.

A room is just a room until someone has a story to tell about it in the morning.

For forty years, the Sunset Marquis has refused to be a hotel. It's a greenroom with a pool. A bar with a zip code. A villa with a door you can close on the twenty-first century and a record collection on the other side of it.

We've added Villas, a Spa, and a restaurant. We have not added a single ounce of restraint. The new rooms do the same thing the old ones always did — they keep your secrets.

Legendary nights begin at Sunset. They always have. That's not a tagline, that's a reservation.

Launch deckTrade showTV scriptNaming

Built the brand from zero. Gaming NIC that did half the marketing in its name. Launched at NAMM-adjacent. Packaging, TV, heat sink.

Launch deck · Slide 01

Lag is a feeling. So is winning. We put a card in the box that decides which one you have.

A pitch deck opening line. One sentence that earned the next fifty-nine.

Most network cards are accountants. They move packets. They don't care whose packets, or in what order, or whether you just got headshot trying to explain it.

This one cares. This one has opinions. This one is named Killer, which ought to tell you something.

The rest of this deck is about how we built it, what it does, and why gamers will tell each other about it before we ever buy an ad.

Traveler Guitar
Product copyDealer kitNAMM deck

Proprietary in-body tuning system. Instruments on Mount Everest and on submarines. Seven launches. Five NAMM booths. One voice.

Product page · Hero copy

Full scale. Full tone. Half the overhead bin.

An instrument shaped like a question: where else could you take one?

We built this for the musician who treats a guitar the way a climber treats a tent — a thing you carry, not a thing you park.

In-body tuning means the headstock is out of the way and the case is shorter than your carry-on. Full twenty-two-fret scale means the instrument is still an instrument. Not a travel accessory. Not a compromise. Not a cute idea.

Ours have been played on Mount Everest. And on submarines. We will accept photographic evidence of both, which is another way of saying: send pictures.

05 ·Voice systems

Brand voice is a rulebook, not a vibe.

Voice & tone
Brand books
Copy decks
SKILL.md
Persona files
Style guides

Every brand I've launched has a written voice system — a short rulebook that tells anyone writing for the brand what it sounds like, what it doesn't, and why.

A good voice system has three things: a sentence only this brand could say, a sentence no other brand would say, and a set of moves that make the first easier to write than the second.

"If anyone else could write it, we didn't."

The same discipline now runs my AI work. Persona files, system prompts, and SKILL.md documentation are brand voice systems for models — the rulebook that keeps an agent on-voice across a thousand generations.
