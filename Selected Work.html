02 Sunset Marquis Campaign · 2019—2024

A hideaway with a Grammy on the nightstand.

West Hollywood's rock-and-roll hotel in two registers — a Magritte-style visual pun for the quiet confident reader, and a full-bleed double-exposure that lets the property's gardens live inside the guest. Same brand, two tempos.
